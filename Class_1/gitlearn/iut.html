Islamic University of Technology

Activities

Academic Programs

The university offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, technology, and business.

Research and Development

Students and faculty are actively involved in research projects that contribute to technological advancements and innovation.

Workshops and Seminars

Regular workshops and seminars are conducted to enhance the skills and knowledge of students and staff.

Extracurricular Activities

The university encourages participation in sports, cultural events, and clubs to promote overall development.

Community Service

Various community service programs are organized to instill a sense of social responsibility among students.
